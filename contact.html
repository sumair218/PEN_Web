Frequently Asked Questions

How can I partner with PEN?

We welcome partnerships with organizations that share our mission. Please reach out to us via the contact form or email us directly at partnerships@pen.org to discuss potential collaboration opportunities.

Do you offer volunteer opportunities?

Yes, we regularly need volunteers for various projects. Please specify your area of interest and availability in your message, and our team will get back to you with current opportunities.

How can I support PEN's work financially?

We appreciate any financial support for our initiatives. You can make a donation through bank transfer or by visiting our office. For details, please contact us directly.

Does PEN offer training programs?

Yes, we regularly conduct training programs in journalism, community development, and digital skills. Check our Projects page for upcoming training opportunities or contact us for more information.

About PEN

Publishing Extension Network is a registered civil society organization working in Gilgit-Baltistan since 1997 to strengthen local voices and promote sustainable development.
