Who We Are

PEN was founded in 1997 with the objective of strengthening local voices in the development and strives to empower the women and youth through mobilization, advocacy, information & knowledge management, capacity enhancement and research to promote sustainable human development.

Publishing Extension Network (PEN) is a registered non-governmental organization with the Government of Pakistan under the voluntary social welfare agencies ordinance 1961.

Our Mission

At Publishing Extension Network, we aim to empower communities through development communication, capacity building, advocacy and knowledge management. We work to create lasting change by working with local communities, institutions, and grassroots organizations.
